Discover History

One of the Four Great Academies

Established in 484 AD, the Songyang Academy is a masterpiece of traditional Chinese architecture and a symbol of the Confucian spirit. It served as a premier institution for higher learning, attracting great scholars like the Neo-Confucian masters Cheng Hao and Cheng Yi.

🌲

The General's Cypress

Witness the "General's Cypress" (General Bai), an ancient cypress tree over 4,500 years old. Its twisted branches tell the story of time, standing as a living fossil of the academy.

📜

Confucian Heritage

Explore the lecture halls where the "Cheng-Zhu School" of philosophy was born. The academy was a cradle for Neo-Confucianism, influencing Chinese thought for centuries.

🏛️

Imperial Steles

Admire the massive steles, including the "Stele of Imperial Conferring," which stands as a testament to the high status the academy held in the eyes of emperors.
