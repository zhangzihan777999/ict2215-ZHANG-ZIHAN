The world's largest dramatic cluster. A journey through 5,000 years of Henan's soul, built from rammed earth and wheat.

Enter the City of Fantasy

More Than a Theme Park

Designed by the renowned architect Wang Chaoge, "Only Henan" is not just a tourist destination—it is a meditation on land, history, and the people of the Central Plains. The entire complex is constructed with rammed earth, creating a labyrinth of walls that tells the story of Chinese civilization.

The Theaters

Immersive experiences that blur the line between audience and performer

Main Feature

The Red Rice Theater

A grand narrative spanning centuries. Witness the struggles and triumphs of the people of the Central Plains, centered around the story of a bowl of rice and the cycles of life.

Immersive

The Railway Station Theater

Step back into the 1930s. This theater recreates a bustling railway station during wartime, allowing you to walk through history and interact with the characters.

Visual Spectacle

The Fantasy City

An open-air theatrical experience where the architecture itself is the stage. The high rammed earth walls create a maze of light, shadow, and history.

"The earth is the stage, and history is the script."
— Wang Chaoge, Chief Director
