Enter the Temple

A Sanctuary of Peace & Power

Located on the western side of Songshan Mountain in Dengfeng, the Shaolin Temple is not just a tourist destination—it is a pilgrimage site for martial artists worldwide. Established in 495 AD, it is the birthplace of Zen Buddhism and the cradle of Shaolin Kung Fu.

⛩️

The Ancient Architecture

Walk through the Shanhaiguan Gate and the Mahavira Hall. The temple complex is filled with steles, murals, and ancient cypress trees that have witnessed over 1,500 years of history.

🥋

Shaolin Kung Fu

Witness the legendary martial arts performances. The monks' movements are a blend of physical strength and Zen philosophy—fast as the wind, steady as the mountain.

🧘

Zen Meditation

Beyond the fighting, Shaolin is a place of worship. Experience the silence of the meditation halls and learn about Bodhidharma, the founder of Zen.
